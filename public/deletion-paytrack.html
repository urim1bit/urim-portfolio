Account & Data Deletion

At PayTrack, we prioritize your data privacy. Because the app uses a "Local-First" architecture with encrypted cloud synchronization, your work logs and photos are never stored on our private servers.

Data Management & Deletion

1. In-App "Wipe Data" (Recommended)

PayTrack provides a self-service deletion path directly within the application:

  • Navigate to Profile & Settings.
  • Select Data Management.
  • Tap Proceed To Wipe Data.

This action permanently deletes your local records and sends a command to wipe your encrypted backups from the cloud.

2. About Cloud Storage

PayTrack stores your backups in a Secure Hidden App Folder within your personal Google Drive. This folder is only accessible by the app to ensure your financial logs remain private and protected from accidental deletion.

If you have uninstalled the app and wish to ensure all metadata and hidden cloud records are cleared, please proceed to the Account Revocation section below.

Account Revocation Request

If you wish to revoke your Google Sign-In authorization and request a full deletion of your user profile record (UID), please contact us:

Email: dev.paytrack@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion - PayTrack

Upon receiving your request, we will remove your Firebase Authentication record within 7 business days.
