Account Deletion

Transparent data management for PayTrack users.

In accordance with Google Play safety standards, we provide clear methods for users to delete their account and all associated personal data.

smartphoneSelf-Service Deletion

The most direct way to wipe your data is through the PayTrack App. This fulfills the requirement for a self-service deletion path:

Profile & Settings → Data Management → Proceed To Wipe Data

Result: This action permanently clears your local device records and wipes your encrypted hidden AppData folder on Google Drive.

cloud_offManual Revocation

If you have already uninstalled the application and wish to request a manual deletion of your Firebase UID record and Google account link, please contact us:

Email: dev.paytrack@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - PayTrack

infoData Retention Policy

PayTrack utilizes a Secure Hidden App Folder on Google Drive. This data is not visible to you in the standard Drive interface. To ensure a full cleanup of these hidden records, please use the in-app wipe tool before uninstalling.
