Privacy Policy

PayTrack

Last updated: 23-02-2026

Thank you for using our app. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

If you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In via Firebase Authentication, we may access basic account information such as your email address and a unique user ID. Login is optional and only used to enable backup and sync features.

2. User Content (Photos and Work Logs)

The app allows you to take photos, import images from your gallery, and record working hours or notes. This content is stored directly in your Google Drive account for backup and synchronization. We do not store this data on our own servers.

3. Notifications

The app may send reminders to help you log your working hours. These notifications are used solely for app functionality.

How We Use Information

We use the information only to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Save and sync your photos and work logs to your Google Drive
  • Provide reminders and core app features
  • Maintain and improve app functionality

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is transmitted only to services necessary for app functionality, such as Google services (Firebase Authentication and Google Drive).

Data Storage

All user content (photos and work logs) is stored in your personal Google Drive. We do not maintain copies on our servers.

Data Security

Data is transmitted securely using encryption provided by Google services.

Your Choices and Data Deletion

  • Use the app without signing in
  • Delete your photos and logs from your Google Drive at any time
  • Stop using the app to discontinue data processing

Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or app listing.
