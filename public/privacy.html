Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23-02-2026

Thank you for using PayTrack. Your privacy is important to us. We operate on a local-first philosophy to ensure your financial data remains under your exclusive control.

fingerprintInformation We Collect

Account Information: If you utilize Google Sign-In, we access your email and a unique User ID via Firebase Authentication for secure synchronization. Login is optional.

User Content: Photos and work logs are stored in a secure, hidden AppData folder on your Google Drive. We do not store this data on our own servers.

Notifications: The app may send reminders to help you log your working hours, used solely for app functionality.

settingsHow We Use Information

  • Authenticate your account and verify user identity
  • Synchronize work logs and photos to your Google Drive
  • Provide core functionality and reminders
  • Maintain and improve app performance

lock_personData Sharing & Security

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. All data transmissions are encrypted using standard protocols provided by Google (Firebase and Drive API).

delete_sweepYour Choice & Deletion

You maintain full authority over your data. You can:

  • Use the app without signing in
  • Delete photos and logs from your Drive at any time
  • Use the in-app "Proceed To Wipe Data" tool to clear local and cloud records
