The Story Behind PayTrack

March 03, 2026 • 5 min read

Pull up a chair, my friend, and let me tell you a little story about what I’ve been up to over the last month.

The story begins just a few weeks ago, in the chilly days of January 2026. By day, I put in long, hard hours working for a construction company called FG Construction / Elzan. Now, let me get one thing straight right away—I didn't start this project because I didn't trust my bosses. Not at all. I did it simply because I wanted to understand my own life. I wanted to know exactly where I stood with my taxes and my net income. I just wanted a clear picture of what my hard work actually translated to at the end of the month.

The Little Laptop That Could

For the longest time, I had this dream of making an app. I always wanted to do it, but I never quite had the courage. I was so skeptical about my own abilities. "Can I really do this?" I’d ask myself. But then, a little light sparked inside my mind, and I finally decided to listen to it.

I didn't have fancy equipment. I just went out and bought a cheap, humble little laptop—just barely strong enough to open up Android Studio without freezing. I took a deep breath, and I began to code using Flutter.

Midnight Oil and Burning Eyes

What followed was one month of absolute madness. By day, I was giving my all to the construction site, and by night, I was giving my soul to that glowing screen. I coded for heavy, endless hours. I worked on it until I felt like I was going blind, squinting at lines of code while the rest of the world was fast asleep.

At first, it was just a secret project for myself, just a personal tool to keep my own records straight. But as the nights went on, I couldn't stop. I kept implementing a new feature here, a little tweak there. Before I knew it, the app had become something really neat. It was simple, it was clear, and yet, it was incredibly powerful.

Sharing the Spark

Sitting there in the dark, rubbing my tired eyes, it suddenly hit me: Maybe I’m not the only one. Maybe someone else out there is looking for an app exactly like this.

I'm not expecting this app to miraculously change my life or hand me a brighter future on a silver platter. At the end of the day, it's just a really nice, practical tool. I'm already profiting from using it in my own daily routine, and I realized that maybe someone else working hard out there could benefit from it, too.

To this day, no one truly knows just how happy I am that I finally took the leap. That guy who was too scared to try, who doubted his own abilities? He finally built something with his own two hands.

So, my friend, if you ever feel that little spark inside you... don't ignore it. You never know what useful little thing you might end up bringing into the world.
