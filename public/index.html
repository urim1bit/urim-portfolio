Passionate about building mobile experiences

I’m an Android developer with a strong passion for building practical and meaningful applications. I enjoy turning ideas into real digital solutions that help people in their everyday lives. My first app, PayTrack, is a project I’m especially proud of. PayTrack allows users to log their work hours, automatically calculate their earnings, deduct taxes, and clearly display the real net salary amount. I created it to solve a simple but important problem — understanding exactly how much you truly earn after deductions.



Building PayTrack was an incredibly exciting milestone for me. Starting with limited experience and pushing myself to learn, experiment, and improve along the way made the journey even more rewarding. Seeing my app go from just an idea to a fully functional product gave me a huge boost of motivation and confidence.


I’m genuinely excited about this path as an Android developer, and this is only the beginning. I’m committed to continuous learning, improving my skills, and building apps that make a real difference
