PRIVACY

Your data, your device.

PayTrack never sends your data to any server. Everything lives on your phone.

PIN Lock
Protect your earnings data with a PIN lock. Only you can open the app on your device.

Google Drive Backup
Optionally back up your shift data to your personal Google Drive — encrypted and only accessible by you.

100% Offline
No network calls, no analytics, no tracking. PayTrack works entirely offline. Your data never leaves your phone.

Built on Room DB (Android's official SQLite ORM). All data is stored securely on-device with no cloud dependency.
