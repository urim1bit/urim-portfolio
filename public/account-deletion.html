Data Deletion

User Rights • Managed Control • Data Sovereignty

At PayTrack, we believe you should have absolute authority over your information. This page provides clear instructions on how to exercise your right to be forgotten.

delete_foreverSelf-Service Deletion

In-App Deletion: The most efficient way to remove your data is via the app settings. Navigate to Settings > Data Management and select "Proceed To Wipe Data".

Cloud Removal: Using the in-app tool will automatically clear all work logs and photos from the hidden AppData folder on your Google Drive.

infoWhat is Deleted?

When you trigger a data wipe, the following information is permanently removed:

• All Net/Gross earning records and work logs.

• All attached receipt photos and project screenshots.

• All custom tax percentages and project billing rates.

• All localized session data and foreground service logs.

warning Irreversible Action

Deletion is permanent. Once your data is wiped from your local device and Google Drive AppData folder, it cannot be recovered by PayTrack, as we do not maintain server-side backups of your content.
